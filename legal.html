Privacy & Accessibility

This is a personal website. It is built to respect your privacy and to be usable by everyone.

Privacy

This site is a personal portfolio. It does not collect, store, or sell personal information, and it does not use cookies, advertising, or analytics or other third-party tracking.

What is stored on your device

The only information kept is your display preferences, your colour theme and text size, saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This stays on your device, is never transmitted to anyone, and is removed if you clear your browser storage.

Fonts

The site loads the Poppins typeface from Google Fonts. When it does, your browser contacts Google's servers, which may process your IP address solely to deliver the font. No cookies are set by this site, and a standard system font is used as a fallback.

External links

Links to LinkedIn lead to third-party websites that are governed by their own privacy policies.

Your choices and contact

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have any question about privacy, you are welcome to reach out via LinkedIn.

Accessibility

Accessibility is a priority for this site, which aims to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Features

  • Semantic HTML with ARIA landmarks for clear structure.
  • Full keyboard navigation, a visible focus indicator, and a skip-to-content link.
  • Colour combinations chosen for sufficient contrast in both light and dark themes.
  • Respect for the operating-system "reduced motion" setting, plus an on-page Motion toggle.
  • Light, dark, and system theme options.
  • Adjustable text size for easier reading.
  • Descriptive alternative text for meaningful images; the decorative animated background is hidden from assistive technology.

Feedback

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. If you encounter any barrier or have a suggestion, please let me know via LinkedIn and I will do my best to put it right.
